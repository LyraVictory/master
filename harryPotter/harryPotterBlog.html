Here you can read all my thoughts about Harry Potter and the fandom. I give opinions and share fun facts I've found. The first posts are about the four houses.

Gryffindor! Where dwell the brave at heart. Their daring, nerve, and chivalry Set Gryffindors apart. Everyone knows Gryffindors are the brave. They can take on any challenge. (well, unless you're Ron Weasley and it involves spiders) Here are some facts about the Gryffindor house

  • Their house crest animal is a lion
  • Their colors are red and gold
  • Gryffindor is the house of fire
  • J.K Rowling is a Gryffindor (Too bad she's not a Ravenclaw)
  • Godric Gryffindor was in favor of muggle-born wizards
  • The sorting hat used to be Godric Gryffindor's fashion accesory
  • Gryffindor often causes hatstalls

    • These facts come from Screenrant.com

Ravenclaw! Where those of wit and learning will always find their kind. The best house of them all. (not at all biased ;P) Ravenclaw is the smartest house, and MY house. People believe that Hermione should've been in Ravenclaw, but the sorting hat put her in Gryffindor because she valued courage over learning. Here are some facts about Ravenclaw:

  • Their house crest animal is an eagle
  • Their colors are blue and bronze
  • Ravenclaw is the house of air
  • The ravenclaw common room is the highest in Hogwarts
  • Ravenclaws have to solve a riddle to get into their common room
  • Professor Quirrel and Professor Lockhart were both Ravenclaws
  • A ravenclaw invented floo powder! (cool!)
  • Moaning Myrtle was a Ravenclaw
  • Ravenclaw is a haven for eccentricity

    • These facts come from Screenrant.com

Hufflepuff! Where they are just and loyal. Those patient Hufflepuffs are true and unafraid of toil. Although I am a Ravenclaw, I have some Hufflepuff in me, too. The Hufflepuffs are hardly mentioned in the books, so you can't help but feel bad for them. They are kind and honest, so they probably don't get into too many crazy shenangians, but they are the calm between the storm. Here are some facts about Hufflepuff:

  • Their house crest animal is a badger
  • Their house colors are yellow and black.
  • The jewels used to track house points for Hufflepuff are diamonds
  • Subjects involving plants or animals appeal to Hufflepuffs
  • Hufflepuff is J.K Rowling's favorite house!
  • The Hufflepuffs have a really interesting common room
  • Helga Hufflepuff welcomed hundreds of house-elves in to kitchens and treated them with much respect, a thousand years before Hermione's S.P.E.W
  • A hufflepuff created Hogsmeade!
  • Hufflepuffs were almost bears instead of badgers

    • These facts come from Screenrant.com

Slytherin! Where those cunning folks use any means to achieve their ends. The lytherin house has a bad rap just because it's known to produce dark wizards, and sure, almost all the death eaters - and their children- were in Slytherin, but there are plenty of decent Slytherins out there, too (*cough, SuperCarlinBrothers, cough*). Here are some facts from Screenrant.com about Slytherin:

  • Their house crest animal is a snake
  • Their house colors are green and silver
  • The Slytherin/Gryffindor rivalry goes way back to the time of Hogwarts' founding
  • Salazar Slytherin helped found the U.S Wizarding school, Ilvermorny.
  • Merlin was a Slytherin
  • Slytherins are really great at occlumency
  • Slytherin has won the house cup more years than any other house
  • Some Slytherins did stay to fight in the Battle of Hogwarts
  • Slytherin is the first and last house mentioned in the series

Hermione Granger's Best Quotes

I think all Harry Potter fans can agree that Hermione is an all around awesome character. She's got the bravery of Gryffindor, the smarts of Ravenclaw, the kindness of Hufflepuff, and she can keep secrets and make deals as well as a Slytherin might. In the movies and books alike, Hermione has had her fair share of great dialouge, so here are my favorite Hermione quotes:

  • "Are you sure that's a real spell , well it's not very good, is it?" Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's stone
  • "It's Levi-OH-sa, not Levi-oh-SAH!" Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's stone
  • "I'm going to bed, before either of you come up with another clever idea to get us killed, or worse, expelled." Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's stone
  • "Me? Books and cleverness. There are more important things: friendship and bravery.” Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone
  • "Fear of a name, only increases fear of the thing itself." Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets
  • "At least no one on the Gryffindor team had to buy their way in, they got in on pure talent!" Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets
  • *Hermione punches Draco* "That felt good." Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban
  • "Just because it's taken you three years to notice, Ron, doesn't mean no one else has spotted I'm a girl. Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire
  • "Just because you have the emotional range of a teaspoon, doesn't mean we all have!" Harry Potter and the Order of the Pheonix
  • After seeing Ron kiss Lavender Brown: "Excuse me, I have to go and vomit" Harry Potter and the Half Blood Prince
  • "Mudblood and proud of it." Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows

Luna Lovegood's Best Lines

Personally, my favorite character out of all of the Harry Potter universe is Luna Lovegood, the quirky, animal loving, Ravenclaw who doesn't quite fit in with everyone else. Luna is basically me, in personality and almost in looks. But here is a list of my all time favorite character's best quips and random outbursts of observations:

  • "I can see them, too. You're just as sane as I am." Harry Potter and the Order of the Pheonix
  • "Things we lose always have a way of coming back to us in the end. If not always in the way we expect." Harry Potter and the Order of the Pheonix
  • "I enjoyed the meetings, too. It was like having friends."Harry Potter and the Half Blood Prince
  • (Book only) "Is that why you dyed your eyebrow, for the party? Should I do mine, too?" Harry Potter and the Half Blood Prince
  • (Movie only) "Daddy, Harry doesn't want to talk to us right now, he's jsut too polite to say no." Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows
  • "That's a curious thing to keep in your sock."Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows
  • SPOILER WARNING! “I think we ought to say something. I’ll go first, shall I? Thank you so much Dobby for rescuing me from that cellar. It’s so unfair that you had to die when you were so good and brave. I’ll always remember what you did for us. I hope you’re happy now.” Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows
  • "It's obvious, Harry, you have to talk to someone who's dead." Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows
  • "I think the answer is that a circle has no beginning." Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows
